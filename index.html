Functionality

A modular self-driving laboratory stack.

PANDA productizes the low-level hardware, control, and data loops that labs usually have to build themselves, so researchers can focus on scientific questions and synthesis goals.

Robotics

CNC gantry experimentation

Automates repeatable experiment execution across 96-well plates without requiring a researcher to manually move between every condition.

Preparation

Integrated fluid handling

Supports the pipetting and material preparation steps needed to turn experimental designs into physical samples.

Measurement

Electrochemistry + optics

Combines electrochemical and optical characterization so every run can produce structured, machine-readable feedback for the next decision.

AI loop

Bayesian optimization

Uses prior results to intelligently select each next experiment, compressing what might take months of manual iteration into days of autonomous lab work.

Data

Clean structured output

Generates experiment records that make it easier to compare conditions, identify promising synthesis windows, and surface optimal synthesis conditions automatically.

Extensible

Modular lab integrations

Designed to support different potentiostats, pipetting systems, and characterization tools, extending beyond the initial conductive polymer synthesis use case.

Why professors care

Run overnight. Learn by morning.

Instead of spending weeks manually testing conditions, a research lab can define the scientific objective and let PANDA run the iterative experiment-measure-optimize loop.

The goal is a turnkey self-driving lab that helps materials science and chemistry teams move faster without building custom hardware or writing low-level control software from scratch.
